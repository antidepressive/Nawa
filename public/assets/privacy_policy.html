Compliance with Saudi Personal Data Protection Law (PDPL)

Nawa AlAthr is committed to full compliance with the Saudi Personal Data Protection Law (Royal Decree M/19) and its Implementing Regulations, effective 14 September 2024.

Your Rights under PDPL

Under PDPL, you have several rights concerning your personal data, including:

  • Right of Access: You may request copies of your personal data.
  • Right to Rectification: You can ask us to correct inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Right to Erasure: You may request the deletion of your data.
  • Right to Restriction: You may request the restriction of data processing under certain conditions.
  • Right to Object: You have the right to object to certain data processing activities, including direct marketing.
  • Right to Data Portability: You may request your data to be transferred to another organization.
  • Withdrawal of Consent: You can withdraw consent for processing at any time.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at privacy@nawa.sa. If unsatisfied, you may lodge a complaint with the Saudi Data and AI Authority (SDAIA).

Legal Basis for Processing Personal Data

We process personal data based on the following legal grounds:

  • Performance of a Contract: To deliver services you have requested or participate in our programs.
  • Legitimate Interests: For improving our services, site security, and communication with you.
  • Consent: For direct marketing and non-essential cookies, only when explicitly granted by you.
  • Legal Obligation: To comply with Saudi laws and regulations.
